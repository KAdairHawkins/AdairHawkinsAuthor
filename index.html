About Me

Hi there! My name is Adair Hawkins and I am a writer working on my debut novel The Roamer. I have a passion for all things creative, and I am excited about any project that gives me the chance to delve into the lives of my characters and spice it up with fantasy!

Connect with me via social media, and view more about The Roamer !

The Roamer

The Roamer is a young adult fantasy about girl forced to use her ability as a shape-shifter to be a princess' body guard. The two become unlikely friends in the face of a plot to overthrow the kingdom, but no one expects the real traitor is hiding within the castle walls. Join Lyna and Brianna in an epic tale of bravery and friendship.
